Things you need to know

The people. And the ability to connect - UWindsor radiates a sense of community.

UWindsor offers more than 200 undergraduate programs. All of our programs combine both academic and relevant experience to prepare you to gain a competitive advantage in the real-world.

At UWindsor, you will have an opportunity to interact with one of our faculties in a moderate to small-class sizes, with a supportive learning experience.

There truly is something for everyone at UWindsor—everything from getting involved with our campus radio to undergrad research to studying abroad.

The University of Windsor has established relationships with many employers locally, nationally and abroad.

Many of those employers that we work with through co-op, internships and other experiential learning programs also hire grads for different roles in their organization.

UWindsor indeed provides you with an education that will see you succeed in your future.
