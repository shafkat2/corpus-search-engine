        Questions? Need Help? 
        There are multiple ways to connect with UWindsor for help 
         
         
         Academic Advising: We're here to help you along the way. Chat with an Academic Advisor. 
         Student Support Services: We offer many student support services that are important to your success. 
         ask.UWindsor Live Chat: Ask current students questions or search our knowledge base articles for answers to commonly asked questions.
