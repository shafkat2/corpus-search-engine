Leddy Library

Access to Study Space, Computer Workstations, and Contactless Pickup is available Monday - Friday 9:00 am - 4:00 pm.

Remote hours of operation are 8:30 am - 4:30 pm from Monday to Friday. Please refer to our homepage and Frequently Asked Questions for the latest updates on our remote services, accessing the library building, and hours of operation. Be sure to login to your account to access all of our resources from off-campus. Changes to our loan policies may cause issues renewing items for some users at this time.
