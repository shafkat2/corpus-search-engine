               Resumption of Parking on Campus  
               As part of the Fall 2021 return to campus, all parking lot operations will resume for Faculty, Staff, Students and Visitors on September 1, 2021.  
               Permits will be pro-rated for the current permit year and be valid from September 1, 2021, to June 30, 2022.  
               There will be no fees for parking until September 1, 2021.   Parking continues to be waived throughout Summer 2021. 
                 
                 
               Parking Office - Currently Closed 
               The Parking Office is currently closed, and all parking transactions are suspended until September 1, 2021.   
               Emails sent to parking@uwindsor.ca,  will be checked periodically during this time.  
               Student Permits for the Fall, 2021 and Winter, 2022 will go onsale exclusively online on July 7, 2021.    
               Click on the Student Tab to get more information. 
               Parking options are available to: 
                
                Students, 
                Faculty and Staff, 
                Visitors and Non-Faculty & Staff 
                People with accessible parking needs. 
                
               The University of Windsor has over 2,800 parking spaces on campus. Access to these lots is controlled by parking permits, meters and gates during enforcement hours (Every day from 7am to 11pm). 
               Payments are accepted by: 
                
                American Express 
                Visa 
                Mastercard 
                Debit Card 
                UWin Card 
                Cash 
                
               Unfortunately, we no longer accept personal cheques.

           Campus Parking Services Joyce Entrepreneurship Centre, 1st Floor (519) 253-3000 Ext. 2413 parking@uwindsor.ca 
           Office hours: Monday - Friday 8:00am to 4:00pm
